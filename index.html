Treatment FAQs

How long does it take to recover from a knee replacement?

For a knee replacement, it can take up to three months for you to return to most activities, and likely six months to one year to fully recover maximal strength and endurance.

I can't travel alone, so can a family member come and help me?

Yes, you can have a family member accompany you to assist during travel and recovery.

Can I see my doctor before I decide, and what about my post-recovery?

You can schedule a consultation before making a decision, and post-recovery care will be guided by your doctor.

How do I share my reports and cases with the clinic of my choice?

You can share your reports via email, online portals, or by visiting the clinic in person.
